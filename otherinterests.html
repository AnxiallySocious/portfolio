I love engaging in clubs when I have the opportunity. I have a reading group that I’m a part of, and I help lead a community group at my local church. I have a volunteer role focused on keeping people connected, and organizing or helping volunteers for community events. I also mentor several young men in my community, which has been immensely fulfilling.

I volunteer for charities when I have the opportunity. One of my most rewarding experiences has been volunteering for the annual fundraising gala for Friends of WPC Nepal. This involvement led to a meaningful trip to Nepal, where I helped establish a safe home and worked directly with women and children rescued from trafficking. This experience was eye-opening, allowing me to gain perspective beyond my first-world bubble.

My leisure time is filled by hiking with friends and writing fiction as well as nonfiction. I enjoy learning to draw as well, but it’s more for personal enjoyment than out of any natural talent. And I play the piano.
